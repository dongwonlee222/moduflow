# Design Prototype Bridge

Design work must stay linked to an issue and spec.

## Outputs

- `design-brief.md`
- `prototype.md`
- screenshots or live URL links
- UX risks
- states and edge cases
- review decision

Use Product Design capabilities when available, especially before building UI-heavy work.
