# Design Prototype Bridge

Design work must stay linked to an issue and spec.

## Outputs

- `design-brief.md`
- `prototype.md`
- screenshots or live URL links
- UX risks
- states and edge cases
- review decision

Use Product Design capabilities when available, especially before building UI-heavy work.

When design or prototype work is expected to hand off into implementation,
surface the relevant frontend QA templates from `templates/frontend-qa/`:
Storybook required states, QA evidence checklist, and when applicable API
contract mapping, MSW fixture catalog, and Playwright smoke matrix.
